Martin Luther King Jr.

The man with a dream

Martin Luther King Jr. delivering his iconic "I Have a Dream" speech in 1963.

Biography & Key Accomplishments

  • 1929 - Born on January 15 in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • 1955 - Led the Montgomery Bus Boycott.
  • 1963 - Delivered the "I Have a Dream" speech during the March on Washington.
  • 1964 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1965 - Played a key role in the Selma to Montgomery marches.
  • 1968 - Assassinated on April 4 in Memphis, Tennessee.
